# Ihram Rules

## Obligations (Wajib)
- Enter Ihram with intention (niyyah).
- Chant Talbiyah: “Labbayk Allahumma labbayk...”

## Prohibitions
- Cutting hair or nails.
- Using scented products.
- Contracting marriage.
- Hunting animals.
- For men: covering the head with fitted cloth.
- For women: covering the face with niqab or wearing gloves.

## Sunnahs of Ihram
- Ghusl before Ihram.
- Wearing white garments for men.
- Two rak’ahs before making intention.

## Invalidations
- Breaking Ihram rules intentionally → requires fidya.
