# Duas for Hajj & Umrah

## Entering the Mosque
- “Allahumma iftah li abwaba rahmatik.”

## Talbiyah
- “Labbayk Allahumma labbayk...”

## Between Yemeni Corner & Black Stone
- “Rabbana atina fi al-dunya hasanah…”

## General Supplications
- Any Qur'an, dhikr, or personal dua.
