# Sa’i Between Safa and Marwah

## 1. After Tawaf
- Perform Sa'i immediately after Tawaf if doing Umrah or Hajj Tamattu'.

## 2. Start at As-Safa
- Face the Ka'bah, raise hands, make dhikr and dua.

## 3. Number of Paths
- 7 paths: Safa → Marwah counts as 1.
- Marwah → Safa counts as 2, and so on until 7.

## 4. Green Light Area (Men)
- Jog lightly between the green markers.

## 5. At Every End
- Stop at Safa or Marwah, raise hands, and make dua.

## 6. Completion
- Ends at Marwah on the 7th lap.
