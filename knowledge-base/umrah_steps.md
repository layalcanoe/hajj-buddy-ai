# Umrah Steps – Summary

1. Enter Ihram + Intention.
2. Recite Talbiyah until reaching Ka'bah.
3. Perform Tawaf (7 rounds).
4. Pray 2 rak’ahs behind Maqam Ibrahim.
5. Drink Zamzam.
6. Perform Sa’i.
7. Shave/Trim hair.
8. Umrah completed.
