# Hajj Steps – Summary (Hajj al-Tamattu’)

## Day 8 – Tarwiyah
- Enter Ihram.
- Go to Mina and pray Dhuhr to Isha shortened.

## Day 9 – Arafah
- Stand at Arafah until sunset.
- Go to Muzdalifah, pray Maghrib + Isha.

## Day 10 – Eid Day
- Throw Jamarat al-Aqabah.
- Sacrifice.
- Shave hair.
- Tawaf al-Ifadah + Sa’i.

## Days 11–13 – Tashreeq
- Throw all three Jamarat each day.

## Farewell Tawaf
- Final tawaf before leaving Makkah.
